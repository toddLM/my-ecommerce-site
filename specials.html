Laci's Custom Build





This is a custom build longboard my daughter and I built from scratch this past year while stuck inside. We glued 8 layers of vinear together in three stages, molded, and shaped it by using a vacuum sealed bag. We cut the design, sanded and varnished. My daughter hand painted the bottom with some art she wanted to use. We mounted the trucks, wheels, and grip tape. She rides it wherever she goes and loves it!!
